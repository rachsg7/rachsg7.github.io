Polaris Weather - Target Audience

Target Audience

The target audience of Polaris Weather is as follows:

  • Who: High school students, college students, professional adults, hikers, bikers, kayakers, etc
  • Age: 14 to 100
  • Income: Income of users is likely to be combined household of $50,000 or greater.
  • Motivation: To get an accurate and up-to-date weather update from a local source.
