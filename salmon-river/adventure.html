Adventure Trips

Level 1 - Beginner

Join us on our beginner's tour if you are a rafting first-timer, are in a group with first-timers, or are bringing children. This easy going adventure is perfect if you are uncertain or wanting to learn more. The two hour tour contains gentle rapids.

Length of trip is 2 hours.
Cost: $75 per person
This trip includes a tour guide and a trip down a river, and a half hour break for snacks. Once you've reached your destination, a bus will be waiting to take the group back.

Level 2 - Medium

Once you've had a few whitewater rafting trips under your belt, you may be ready for more of a challenge. This trip is longer than our beginner's trip, and contains harder rapids to maneuver. Not recommended for kids.

Length of trip is 3 hours.
Cost: $80 per person
This trip includes a tour guide and a trip down the river, with a break for snacks. Once you've reached your destination, a bus will be waiting to take the group back.

Level 3 - Advanced

This trip is recommended for more experienced rafters. It contains trips down the river where the rapids are the strongest.

Length of trip is 3 hours.
Cost: $80 per person
This trip includes a tour guide and a trip down the river, with a break for snacks. Once you've reached your destination, a bus will be waiting to take the group back.
